POPIA Compliance

Our commitment to protecting your personal information and privacy.

1. Introduction

Tickify respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), Act 4 of 2013. This policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with our platform.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect personal information that is necessary to deliver our services efficiently. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Full names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Billing and delivery addresses
  • Device and usage information

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for:

  • Processing your ticket purchases and service requests
  • Providing customer support
  • Improving our platform and user experience
  • Sending updates and promotional communications (with consent)
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We will never sell your personal information. We may share your information with trusted third parties only to fulfill service delivery or where legally obligated. All third parties are bound by confidentiality and data protection agreements.

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures including encryption, firewalls, and access controls to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

6. Your Rights

As a data subject under POPIA, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request corrections to your information
  • Object to processing under certain circumstances
  • Request deletion of your personal data
To exercise your rights, please contact us at privacy@tickify.co.za.

7. Updates to this Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect legal or operational changes. We encourage users to review this page periodically.

8. Contact Information

For any queries regarding POPIA compliance or our data practices, please contact:

Email: privacy@tickify.co.za
