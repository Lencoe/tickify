Privacy Policy & Terms of Use

Your trust matters. Here's how we protect and respect your data while using Tickify.

1. Introduction

Tickify is committed to protecting your personal information. This page explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with our platform, and also outlines the terms under which you access and use our services.

We collect the following types of information:

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data helps us:
